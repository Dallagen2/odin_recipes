Green Hatch Chili Burger

Description

This Green Chili Burger really lets the Hatch Chilis shine through! It's an amazing recipe for summer-time!

Ingredients

  • Brioche Buns
  • Onions
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Roasted Hatch Chilis
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheddar Cheese
  1. Dice up the Hatch Chilis, Onions, and mix them into your Ground Beef alongside salt and pepper.
  2. Portion the meat into 3.5-4oz balls, and flatten them into thin patties.
  3. Get your grill as hot as possible and cook the patties until they have a slight crust and browning, top with cheese and let it melt before removing the patties.
  4. Combine the rest of your Hatch Chilis and Onions very finely and add salt and pepper, lime juice, and olive oil.
  5. Top the burger with your onion and hatch chili mixture, serve with two patties.
